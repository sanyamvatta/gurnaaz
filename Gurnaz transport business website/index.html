About Us

Built for disciplined movement across demanding routes.

Gurnazz Transport LLC has been actively involved in the transportation industry within the UAE for the past several years. The company specializes in providing efficient and reliable transport services, catering to both domestic and cross-border logistics needs.

Our Mission

To provide superior transportation services that ensure safe, timely, and cost-effective delivery of goods, enhancing customer satisfaction and fostering long term relationships.

Our Vision

To become a leading transport company in the UAE, recognized for our commitment to excellence, innovation, and customer satisfaction.

Services

Logistics capability with premium control at every handoff.

Domestic Transport

  • Full Truckload (FTL) and Less Than Truckload (LTL) services across the UAE.
  • Expedited delivery services for urgent shipments.
  • Warehousing and distribution support.
  • Dedicated fleet services for long-term contracts with businesses.

Cross-Border Transport

  • Transportation services to neighboring GCC countries.
  • Assistance with customs clearance and documentation.
  • Real-time tracking of cross-border shipments.
  • Specialized services for temperature-sensitive, high-value, or hazardous goods.

Specialized Transport Services

  • Heavy and oversized cargo transport.
  • Cold chain logistics for perishable goods.
  • Dangerous goods transportation, adhering to international safety standards.
